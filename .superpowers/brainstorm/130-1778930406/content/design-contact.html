Design notes:
  • Left column: 3 info cards (Email, Location, Status) + social link row (GitHub, LinkedIn, Twitter)

  • Input fields get violet border glow on focus

  • Slightly darker section background (#080810) to alternate with hero's #0f0f1a
  • Framer Motion: info cards slide from left, form slides from right on scroll
